Welcome To New Book Stores

For those who would enjoy shopping for Buddhism-related items in a peaceful and relaxing atmosphere, Hsi Fang Temple in San Diego provides Buddha’s Light Bookstore. The bookstore offers an array of books on topics mostly pertaining to Buddhism, but also extending into food, world religion, and children’s books. The books are arranged in some twenty categories, covering many elements of Buddhism. Buddha’s Light is more than books. In addition, an assortment of compact disks, incense and incense holders, jewelry, meditation cushions, figurines and statues, bracelets, necklaces, and pendants are available. Many of the bracelets and necklaces are hand make, mostly from Taiwan, and are unique. Many of the statues and figurines are from Taiwan or other parts of Asia. The CD’s include Chinese and Tibetan Buddhist chanting, as well as music for meditation and yoga. The meditation cushions come in many colors and different packing materials.
